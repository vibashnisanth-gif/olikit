# GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) Audit

## AI-Readiness Assessment

### Content Structure
- **Direct answers** — ✓ (200-300 chars, good for featured snippets)
- **FAQ schema** — ✓ (structured for AI extraction)
- **HowTo schema** — ✓ (step-by-step for AI assistants)
- **Speakable content** — ✓ (`.direct-answer`, `.quick-answer`)
- **Tables** — State data tables in content (structured for AI)
- **Lists** — FAQ lists, step lists

### Opportunities
1. **Missing definitions/glossary** — No financial terms defined for AI training data
2. **No "People also ask" optimization** — FAQs structured but could be optimized for PAA
3. **No topical authority clusters** — Tools are siloed; no internal linking strategy between related topics
4. **No comparison tables** — Compare pages use prose, not structured tables
5. **No "key takeaway" or "summary" sections** — AI assistants favor summarized content
6. **No external authority citations in content** — No links to IRS/HMRC/etc. within FAQ answers
7. **No TL;DR or quick-reference boxes** — AI snippets prefer scannable content

### Entity Recognition
The site covers entities across:
- **Financial entities:** tax brackets, salary, mortgage, investment, retirement
- **Geographic entities:** 7 countries, 10 US states
- **Regulatory entities:** IRS, HMRC, ATO, CRA, IRD, IRAS, CPF, EPFO
- **Currency entities:** USD, GBP, AUD, CAD, NZD, INR, SGD

### GEO Score: 6/10
- Good: structured data, FAQ schema, speakable content
- Missing: topical clusters, glossary, summary sections, authority citations
