## How to Use These Templates

1. **Sign up for HARO** (helpareporter.com), Qwoted, and SourceBottle
2. **Check queries 2-3 times daily** — morning, lunch, evening
3. **Respond within 1 hour** of query posting (journalists have tight deadlines)
4. **Personalize each response** — reference the journalist's specific topic
5. **Include data points** — specific numbers get more attention than vague claims
6. **Link to your report** — always include the URL to your research
7. **Follow up** — if no response in 48 hours, send a brief follow-up
