## Analysis Framework (ready for data)

### Bucket A — Quick Wins
- Position 4–10
- CTR below site average
- High impressions
- **Action**: Optimize title, meta description, rich results

### Bucket B — Near Wins
- Position 11–20
- High impressions
- **Action**: Strengthen content, add FAQs, improve internal links

### Bucket C — CTR Problems
- Position 1–3
- CTR below expected
- **Action**: Investigate title/description/intent mismatch

### Bucket D — Content Gaps
- High impressions, very low clicks
- **Action**: Identify intent mismatch, weak content, missing features
